What Does Accessibility in Higher Education Mean?

Accessibility in Higher Education is the overall concept that all individuals should be able to have access to education regardless if they have a disability or not. It also plays into the concepts of UDL or Universal Design for Learning which is the overall concept that by designing for alternative means of access help to create an inclusive learning environment.
